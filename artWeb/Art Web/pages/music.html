Tips and Tricks for Learning and Being Professional Musician

Add Anticipation to Your Movement:
Music is a vital part of different moments of human life. It spreads happiness and joy in a person’s life. Music is the soul of life and gives immense peace to us. In the words of William Shakespeare, “If music is the food of love, play on, Give me excess of it; that surfeiting, The appetite may sicken, and so die.” Thus, Music helps us in connecting with our souls or real self.

ThINgs you must do while LEARNING ART
  • Ignore every obstacle anyone has ever put in front of you.
  • Set a deadline — and stick to it.
  • Learn to record yourself, and do it whenever and wherever's possible.
  • Use LANDR to master :)
  • Make music everyday – no matter how much time you have.
  • Do as much as you can on your own, see what's missing, THEN get help.
  • Write lyrics with concrete imagery.
  • Record every musical idea you have. Use your iPhone, tape recorder or dictaphone.
  • Keep a pad and paper by your bed.
  • Get a 4-track recorder.
  • Play a piano.
  • Stop reading Pitchfork.com. Make music instead.
  • Set up drums in your living room and Go on tour.
  • Recreate your favorite song.
  • Sing with friends
  • Block internet distraction for a chunk of time with the Freedom app.

Ornaments for Music

Music is a language of diversities. Thus, it has numerous styles of music. Some of them are Classical, Pop, Ghazals, Bhajans, Carnatic, Folk, Khyal, Thumri, Qawwali, Bhangra, Drupad, Dadra, Dhamar, Bandish, Baithak Gana, Sufi, Indo Jazz, Odissi, Tarana, Sugama Sangeet, Bhavageet, etc.
