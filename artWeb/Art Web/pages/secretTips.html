Tips and Tricks for Learning Art
  • Ignore every excuse anyone has ever put in front of you.
  • Set a deadline — and stick to it.
  • Learn to record yourself, and do it whenever and wherever's possible.
  • Use LANDR to master :)
  • Make music everyday – no matter how much time you have.
  • Do as much as you can on your own, see what's missing, THEN get help.
  • Write lyrics with concrete imagery.
  • Record every musical idea you have. Use your iPhone, tape recorder or dictaphone.
  • Keep a pad and paper by your bed.
  • Get a 4-track recorder.
  • Play a piano.
  • Stop reading Pitchfork.com. Make music instead.
  • Set up drums in your living room and Go on tour.
  • Recreate your favorite song.
  • Sing with friends
  • Block internet distraction for a chunk of time with the Freedom app.
